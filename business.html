Distribution

Tam Distribution Channel maintains its competitive advantage by maintaining a profitable, high-quality operation. Building on the fundamentals, the unit aims at mapping the best routes to success while developing and strengthening service capabilities.

We have increased portfolio relevance by expanding and optimizing the installation and activation of EVD (Electronic Value Distribution) solutions across the Kingdom.

Deploying robust FMCG route management techniques to the outlets we service in KSA, Tam operates the largest mobile sales force for both, card Distribution and the latest in electronic services (EVD).

Working with the market leading distribution specialist means relying on the fact that we interpret customer needs and market trends accurately to provide the most relevant technology, services and knowledge to grow our Clients businesses.

Retail

Tam Retail Distribution Channels is a critical development area to attract new and current customer segments. By deploying the right number of stores/outlets featuring a mix of attractive services, Sale provides and tailors to all the needs of the end-users.

Customers seek convenience, quality services, and value in one package. For this reason, we continually strive to exceed their expectations by offering them a total solution derived from substantial Retail expertise and offerings expected from a market-leading organization.

We are focused on increasing Retail channel contribution by diversifying the services mix and increasing customer interaction. By taking a dynamic and advanced approach to retailing Telecommunication products and services, we seek to offer customers a quality lifestyle retailing experience rather than just pure products.

Enterprise

As professional service providers, we understand what it takes to become market leaders and sustain an active drive for our organization. With this in mind, we provide the necessary distribution channels and services for our partners and clients within the enterprise sector and in turn empower business owners to successfully operate their ventures.
