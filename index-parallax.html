COMPANY PROFILE

Tam business was started in early twenties with intention of becoming a biggest conglomerate in sim card distribution. It started as distributer with few sim card distribution company to become the largest distribution company in KSA within 15 years. Tam has foothold in almost every cities of KSA and continue to expand with more branches in every tier 2 amd tier 3 cities. Tam believe in people ease of access and satisfaction.

Today Tam business controlled hundreds of outlets all around KSA with sole intention of serving communities better and efficiently. Our company has facilitated various telecom companies in expanding their business into different part of kingdom through our distribution channel.

Our Mission

From advent of our company , our mission is to become best channel between telecom companies and retail market of kingdom of Saudi Arabia. By creating good network we have become one of the major distributor in kingdom. We are focussed on the maintaining and also expanding the distributor network all over kingdom.
