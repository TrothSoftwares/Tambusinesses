About Us

Everything you need to know about Our Company

COMPANY PROFILE

Tam Business started in early 2000's with the intention of becoming the biggest conglomerate in Sim Card distribution. It began as a distributor with other few sim card distribution companies and became the largest distribution company in KSA within 15 years. Tam has a foothold in almost every cities of KSA and continues to expand with more branches in every tier 2 and tier 3 cities. Tam believes in ease of access and satisfaction for their clients.

Today Tam Business controls hundreds of outlets all around KSA with the sole intention of serving clients better and efficiently. Our company has facilitated various telecom companies in expanding their business into different parts of the Kingdom through our distribution channels.

Our Mission.

From the advent of our company, our mission is to become the best channel between telecom companies and retail market of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. By creating a good network, we have become one of the major distributors in the kingdom. We are focussed on maintaining and expanding our distributors' network all over the country.

Our Vision.

Communication sector has grown massively in the past twenty years and has changed lifestyle of people all over the world. We are proud to be a major part of the Telecom industry in Saudi Arabia. Our Vision is to keep contributing towards telecom industries in serving the people of Kingdom to have a better lifestyle.

Our Goal.

Tam Business was founded with the intention of keeping people connected with new technology from around the world. We believe in providing high standards of service in all steps of our business. Our motto to serve for customer satisfaction makes us stand out from others.
